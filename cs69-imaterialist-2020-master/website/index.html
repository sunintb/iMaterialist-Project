    1 - Data Exploration & Preprocessing

    Our EDA has 2 broad components: to understand
    1) Dataset at the MACRO level: distribution of classes &s; attributes;
    2) Individual images at the MICRO level

    We also implemented the following data pre-processing procedures:
    1) Re-scaling of image to a common dimension (e.g. 256 x 256 squares)
    2) Data augmentation via the imaug package Details can be found in our Jupyter Notebook

    2a - Object Categorization Task

    We evaluated 2 model architectures
    1) Mask RCNN (link to paper)
    2) Stacked Hourglass (link to paper)

    We made use of GPU for our computation. Output example can be found here
    Training-validation losses of our chosen model, MaskRCNN, can be found in this Jupyter Notebook .

    2b - Computational Experiments

    We sought to computationally assess:

    1. Effects of model depth by comparing Resnet-50 vs. Resnet-101

    2. Effect of pre-training with COCO dataset weight

    3. Amount of performance gains via data augmentation

    In all cases, the control variable is the dataset, identical between all experimental runs.
